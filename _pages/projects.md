## Current projects
-  <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/chosuncds-eng/about-us/HK_Project">**Elderly Speech Korea**</a> (Language Biomarkers and AI in Neurocognitive Disorders), Chosun University, National Research Foundation of Korea, 2025 - 2026. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher (HK Research Professor) working on developing NLP and LLM-bases evaluation pipelines for Korean long-form audio data, under the supervision of Professor <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/eonsuk/home">Eon-Suk Ko</a> (principal investigator).
- <a href="https://www.starfle.fr/presentation/">**STAR-FLE**</a> (STrategic Adaptations for better Reading and Text Comprehension in FFL), ANR project (2024 – 2027). I am responsable of annotating multi-word expressions in texts written by learners of French. Principal investigator: Professor <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=O9wabiwAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao">Amalia Todirascu</a>.
- **UniDive** (Universality, diversity and idiosyncrasy in language technology), COST Action, CA21167, (2022 – 2026).  I am a researcher in charge of annotating multi-word expressions in Romanian according to the <a href="https://parsemefr.lis-lab.fr/parseme-st-guidelines/1.3/?page=home">PARSEME</a> guide. UniDive principal investigators are: <a href="https://perso.limsi.fr/savary/">Agata Savary</a> (LISN) et <a href="https://ufal.mff.cuni.cz/daniel-zeman">Daniel Zeman</a> (Charles University).

## Past projects
- **ClinReact** (Lorraine Université d’Excellence, 2023 – 2025). As a postdoc, I was in charge of developing generative AI tools for personalized medicine and scientific research on brain cancer using LLMs and SLMs (Large and Small Language Models). I worked in an interdisciplinary team, under the supervision of Professor Mathieu Constant (Natural Language Processing (NLP), ATILF), Professor Marianne Clausel (mathematics, IECL) and Professor Hélène Dumond (molecular biology, CRAN).
- **ADAPTMED** (Resources for Medical Text Adaptation and Simplification, 2023 – 2024). As a research engineer I worked on on the a hybrid model for analyzing medical data on the Covid-19 epidemic. Principal investigator of the project: Professor <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=O9wabiwAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao">Amalia Todirascu</a>.
- **SimpleApprenant** (Proposing tools to tackle learning difficulties of French as a foreign language, 2017-2019). As a linguistics intern, I was responsible for analyzing and annotating texts written by learners of French, under the supervision of Professor <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=O9wabiwAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao">Amalia Todirascu</a>.
